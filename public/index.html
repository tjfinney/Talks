This is the Day

Psalm 118 is a processional hymn for the Feast of Tabernacles, when the Hebrews remember God’s provision during their Exodus out of Egypt. Like many Psalms, this one has a call and response structure. According to Wikipedia,

In music, call and response is a compositional technique, often a succession of two distinct phrases that works like a conversation in music. One musician offers a phrase, and a second player answers with a direct commentary or response.

To Jay

Here is a letter written to a teenager named Jay, who asked for a copy of the Bible.

To Jay

Dear Jay,

I hope that this Bible will be a blessing to you. If you read it you will be blessed. It is a good book and a big book – sixty or so books in fact. There are 39 books in the Old Testament and 27 in the New. That is a lot to get through. You don’t have to start at the beginning and read all the way through. If it was me, I would start with one of the Gospels. Mark is a good place to start.
