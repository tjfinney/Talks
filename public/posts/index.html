Introduction

Today we will look at John chapter 1, verse 1, which starts off with the Greek words for In the beginning. About John

John is one of the twelve apostles, son of Zebedee and Salome. We don’t know much about Zebedee but we do know about Salome. She was one of the women present at the crucifixion of Jesus (see Mark 15.40 and Matthew 27.56) who were also first to discover Christ’s empty tomb (see Mark 16.1-6). John’s brother was the Apostle James. Jesus called James and John the sons of thunder. Jesus told them off for wanting to call down fire on an unwelcoming Samaritan village.
